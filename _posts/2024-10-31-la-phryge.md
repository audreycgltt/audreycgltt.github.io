> LA PHRYYYYYYYYYYYYYGE!

The Olympics games of Paris 2024 were amazing, so was the official mascot : la Phryge!

Kinda obsessed by it, among the insane amount of merch I got of it, I decided to create a costume of it for Halloween. With a twist: reactive LEDs!

The eyes and the mouth are on a 8*32 leds matrix. Behind the hood, an arduino and an MPU6050 to make the eyes following my rotations. 

Unfortunately, I usually spend more time doing my projects than actually thinking of taking photos and videos of it.
